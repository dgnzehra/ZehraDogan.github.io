HR Data Analytics in PowerBI

Analyzing HR Data to Gain Insights into Workforce Trends and Optimization Strategies

The HR Data Analytics project on Power BI centers around analyzing employee attendance, work-from-home patterns, and sick leave trends to provide valuable insights for optimizing workforce management and employee well-being. Leveraging Power BI's visualization capabilities, this project aims to enhance decision-making processes within the HR department.

Data Acquisition and Integration:

  • Collect and integrate relevant HR data, including employee attendance records, work-from-home requests, and sick leave data, from diverse sources into a unified dataset for analysis.

Data Cleaning and Transformation:

  • Apply data cleaning techniques to address inconsistencies, missing values, and outliers. Transform the data to ensure compatibility for meaningful analysis in Power BI.

Exploratory Data Analysis (EDA):

  • Perform an in-depth EDA to understand the distribution of attendance records, work-from-home patterns, and sick leave instances. Identify outliers, trends, and potential correlations among these variables.

Dashboard Creation:

  • Compile the findings into a user-friendly Power BI dashboard, providing HR professionals and management with a centralized platform for real-time monitoring and decision-making.
