Sales Data Analytics

Python Sales Data Analysis: Extracting Insights, Visualizing Trends, and Uncovering Product Patterns

The sales data analysis project involves importing and cleaning sales data using Python's pandas library. The steps include merging monthly data into a single CSV file, cleaning up NaN values, removing irrelevant rows, converting columns to the correct data types, and adding additional columns such as month, sales, and city. The project also includes visualizations using matplotlib to analyze sales trends by month, city, and time of day. Furthermore, it explores products frequently sold together and identifies the best-selling products, providing insights into quantity ordered and pricing.

Data Consolidation:

  • Multiple CSV files containing sales data for 12 months are merged into a single CSV file using Pandas.

Data Cleaning:

  • Rows with missing values are dropped to ensure data integrity.

Sales Insights:

  • Identify the best sales month through summarization and visualization.
  • Determine the city with the highest sales using aggregation and plotting.

Optimal Advertisement Timing:

  • Extract hour and minute from the order date to analyze the best times for displaying advertisements.

Product Combinations:

  • Find products frequently sold together, providing insights into customer purchasing patterns.

Product Performance:

  • Analyze individual product performance, including quantity ordered and average prices.
  • Visualize the data to identify best-selling products and their pricing trends.

By following these steps, the project provides a concise yet comprehensive analysis of sales data, offering valuable insights for decision-making.
